Workshop & Tutorials

  • Workshops
  • Tutorials
  • Fest Albums

  • BASIC & ADVANCED PHOTOGRAPHY

    Photography is all about light . It is not the only way to capture the world, but it certainly one of the most effective. These workshops are aimed to provide the basic concepts of shutter, aperture ,ISO, exposure , white balance.etc.

  • SCIENCE FILM MAKING WORKSHOP

    Filmmaking is an art of portraying your imaginations in a moving picture. These workshops involves a number of discrete stages including an initial story, idea , or commission , through screenwriting , casting, shooting, sound recording and the screening the finished product.

  • GRAPHIC DESIGNING WORKSHOP

    “A designer knows he has achieved perfection not when there is nothing left to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.” We have a bunch of people on our team proficient in Photoshop, Illustrator and Lightroom, who make mind blowing pieces of photo manipulation. They actively conduct workshops to teach the same.

  • BASIC DESIGNING

    Creating beautiful design is about more than inspiration or a great idea, it’s about understanding the fundamentals of the subject. The rules of lines, colors, shape, space , textures etc are the most important components for designing.

  • CAMERA LENS

    A camera lens (also known as photographic lens or photographic objective) is an optical lens or assembly of lenses used in conjunction with a camera body and mechanism to make images of objects either on photographic film or on other media capable of storing an image chemically or electronically.These tutorials will provide you everything you need to know about camera lens.

  • HOW DSLR WORKS?

    If you consider a beginner who is unsure of how to make the most of your camera, these tutorials are designed for you. You will know about the modes, ISO , exposure triangle and many more through these tutorials. These steps can really help you in entering the world of photography.

  • PANTHEON - TECH FEST

    Find the perfect tech fest photographs and coverages of the annual tech fest of BIT MESRA. It showcase, the cutting edge technologies and innovations from various tech fields.

  • VAJRA - ANNUAL SPORTS FEST

    It is easy to capture the still objects , but when it comes to moving objects it is a dire situation. Come and see the zeal and enthusiasm of the participants of Vajra (annual sports fest of BIT MESRA). The emotions of sportsmanship and competitive nature is very well portrayed through these photographs.

  • BITOTSAV - ANNUAL CULTURAL FEST

    If photography is writing with light, then low light photography is like writing a novel with a half inch pencil stub. Glance at the wonderful art gallery of the cultural fest BITOTSAV , both in low light and glazed light. The vibrant colors are so beautifully portrayed.
